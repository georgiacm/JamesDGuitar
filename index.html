About me

I have been playing guitar for 15 years now and teaching for about 10 years to people of many ages, abilities and styles. I started playing guitar at the age of 8 and have picked up many different styles of music along the way including blues, rock, jazz, funk, country, fusion and pop.

Education & Style

I hold a BA Honours Degree in Commercial Music from BIMM Dublin where I've gained a lot of experience and knowledge on guitar techniciques, styles and theory.

I tailor my lessons to suit you and your goals for learning the instrument. No style or technique is out of bounds. You’ll be learning in a relaxed, fun and productive environment from an extremely patient and passionate tutor.

Acoustic Guitar

Acoustic guitar can be found in virtually every genre of guitar music, and it's the most widely played type of guitar in the world.

Simple or complex, joyful or melancholic, fragile or powerful, the acoustic guitar has a very distinct and unmistakable timbre.
